A user opens the website in their browser. They see a login screen.
They login. Now the user sees screen asking them to enter a lobby name.
They enter a lobby name. They are taken to a Quiz screen. They see a list
of users in the lobby on the left side of the screen. They click
a ready button. Above the list of users a counter displays how many players
are ready. Once everyone in the lobby clicks ready, they see a new screen.
They are asked to enter a question, one correct answer and three wrong
answers. They submit their question. Once everyone submitted their
questions, the game starts. They answer questions and see the other
players scores as well as their own on the left. The game ends after all
questions have been answered. A new screen appears showing the results.
They click finished and are taken back to the ready menu.
